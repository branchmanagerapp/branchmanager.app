Real tree workflows

Built for the way tree jobs actually happen.

Generic field-service apps don't understand climbing rates, hazard assessment, or the difference between a routine prune and a crane removal. Here's how Branch Manager handles the work you actually do.

Storm cleanup & emergency response

Phone rings at 11pm with a tree on the roof. Triage the call, send a same-night SMS confirmation, geofence the property for the 6am crew arrival, and have insurance-ready before/after photos on the customer's portal by lunch.

Inbound call → Request created → Same-night SMS → Crew dispatch → Photos auto-link → Invoice with insurance line items

Hazard tree assessment

ISA-style Tree Risk Assessment Qualification (TRAQ) workflow. Mark trees on the property map with risk class, target rating, recommended action. Save as a reusable assessment for repeat clients (HOAs, municipalities).

Site visit → Tree-by-tree TRAQ scoring → PDF report → Quote with prioritized line items → Multi-phase work plan

Removal + stump grind + cleanup

Multi-line quotes with photos per tree. Climber rate, ground rate, equipment time, debris haul, and stump grinding as separate line items. Optional add-ons (wood chips left, logs cut to firewood length) at quote time.

Quote with photos per tree → Customer approves online → Crew assigned by skill → Job complete → Auto-invoice

Recurring municipal & HOA contracts

Set up recurring service agreements (annual deep-prune, quarterly hazard inspection, monthly debris pickup). Auto-generates jobs on schedule, invoices at the end of each cycle. Custom branding for client-facing PDFs.

Contract template → Auto-create recurring jobs → Crew dispatch → Per-cycle invoice → Annual summary report

Crew time tracking + Pre-Trip

Each climber and ground crew clocks in via the field app. Pre-Trip inspection signs off the truck before leaving the yard. Time data drives payroll, job costing, and the Salesperson Performance report.

Crew arrives → Pre-Trip inspection → Clock in → On-site time tracked → Job complete → Auto-payroll

Equipment maintenance & OEM parts

Track every chipper, climber rope, saddle, chainsaw, and bucket truck. OEM part numbers stored per piece. When the Bandit 254T needs a new clutch, the link to Messick's order page is one tap away.

Hour-meter triggers task → Tech logs service → OEM part link → Order from Messick's / R&L → Auto-update next-service interval

Insurance & compliance dashboard

WC, GL, auto, umbrella, employee disability, ANSI Z133 training records, ISA member ID, TCIA member ID, county HIC licenses (Westchester, Putnam, etc.), USDOT, MCS-150, NY DOS biennial — all in one dashboard with renewal alerts.

Cert uploaded → Renewal date set → Auto-alert at 90/60/30 days → One-tap renewal portal link → Cert refreshed

Frequently asked questions

Can I import from Jobber?

Yes — direct importer for Jobber CSV exports (clients, jobs, invoices, quotes, payments). First import included in onboarding. We've migrated tree services off Jobber, ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, and Service Fusion.

Does the app handle ANSI Z133 / ISA workflows?

Yes. ISA Tree Risk Assessment Qualification (TRAQ) workflow is built in for hazard tree assessments. ANSI Z133 training records are tracked under Insurance & Compliance with renewal alerts. TCIA membership and certified arborist credentials are first-class fields.

Can the customer portal show before/after photos?

Yes. Every photo your crew snaps on the job auto-links to the job and the customer's portal. They can download high-res copies, share to Facebook, or print. Great for repeat business — they remember you next storm.

What about payment plans for $10k+ removals?

Stripe-backed payment plans (3, 6, or 12 payments). Customer signs a deposit + plan agreement; you collect the deposit up front; Stripe auto-bills the balance per the schedule. Great for big crane jobs.

Do you handle municipal & HOA contracts?

Yes — recurring service agreements with auto-generated jobs and per-cycle invoices. Custom-branded PDFs for client-facing reports. Annual summary report for renewals.

How does crew time tracking work?

Each crew member clocks in via the field app. Pre-Trip inspection signs off the truck. Time data drives payroll, job costing, and the Salesperson Performance report.

Do I have to use Bouncie?

No. Bouncie is an optional fleet add-on. Branch Manager works fine without GPS hardware — you just won't have live truck positions on the dispatch map.

What does the 14-day trial include?

Full access to every feature of the plan you pick. No credit card required. At day 14 you either pick a plan and add a card, or your account pauses (data preserved 30 days).
