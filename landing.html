Frequently asked questions

Do I need to install anything?

No. The web app runs in any modern browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge. On iOS or Android you can "Add to Home Screen" for an app-like icon. The native iOS app is coming to the App Store shortly; current beta users access it via TestFlight.

Can I import data from legacy field-service software?

Yes. We have an importer for Jobber CSV exports (clients, jobs, invoices, quotes, payments). For other systems, send us a CSV of your data and we'll handle it. First import is included in onboarding.

What does the 14-day trial include?

Full access to every feature of the plan you pick. No credit card required. At day 14 you either pick a plan and add a card, or your account pauses (data preserved for 30 more days in case you change your mind).

How does payment processing work?

Branch Manager uses Stripe for checkout, deposits, and tipping. Stripe rates apply (currently 2.9% + 30¢ per online card). ACH is 0.8% capped at $5. We don't take a cut.

What about my customers' data privacy?

Each tenant's data is fully isolated by Postgres row-level security. We don't sell, share, or look at customer data. SOC 2 compliance roadmap is in progress.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel from Settings → Subscription. Service runs through the end of the paid period. Export all your data as CSV before you go; you own your data.

Do I have to use Bouncie?

No. Bouncie is an optional fleet-tracking add-on. Branch Manager works fine without any GPS hardware — you just won't have live truck positions on the dispatch map.

What if I'm not a tree service?

Branch Manager is purpose-built for tree services and the language reflects that (limbs, removals, stump grinding, climber rates). Adjacent trades (landscaping, hardscaping) work but won't be a perfect fit. Talk to us if you're on the fence.
